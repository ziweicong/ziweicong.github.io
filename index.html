Ziwei Cong

Department of Marketing • Business School • The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology
LSK4017, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

I am an empirical marketing scientist working on large-scale causal behavior analytics, with a particular interest in the production and consumption of digital content. My current research focuses on questions related to monetizing user generated content and the impact of platform policies (e.g., recommendation algorithms) on user behavior. In a broad sense, the goal of my research is to inform decision making of digital content creators and platforms by combining state-of-the-art machine learning methods with causal inference.

I am fortuate to have my training supervised by Ying Zhao and Jia Liu (co-advisors) at HKUST. Outside of HKUST, I am also working with Puneet Manchanda (Stephen M. Ross School of Business, University of Michigan). I have worked in Zhihu as a research intern in 2017.

Before joining HKUST, I received a Bachelor and Mphil of Economics from Renmin University of China (2013, 2016).

I am on the job market for the 2021-2022 academic year and will be available for interviews.
